## 1. App Logic: How to Use Planetary Energy with Lunar Mansions

**Step 1 – Determine active planet**

For any given time block (e.g. current lunar mansion, planetary hour, or selected focus):

1. Identify the **ruling planet**:

   * via lunar mansion ruler, or
   * via planetary hour, or
   * via user selection (manual override).

2. Load the **planet profile** from a static data table:

   * `name`
   * `keywords`
   * `colors`
   * `scents`
   * `supportive_foods`
   * `avoid_foods`
   * `fasting_instruction`
   * `service_instruction`
   * `behavior_focus`

**Step 2 – Show a simple protocol**

For the active planet, present a **daily protocol**:

1. **Color cue** – what to wear/use.
2. **Scent cue** – oil/incense suggestion.
3. **Food cue** – what to emphasize + what to avoid.
4. **Fasting cue** – what to consciously refrain from that day.
5. **Service cue** – 1 concrete action “in service” to that planet.
6. **Behavior focus** – one sentence of how to behave/align.

**Step 3 – Optional integration with lunar mansions**

For each lunar mansion:

1. Map mansion → ruling planet.
2. Auto-apply that planet’s protocol as the **default** for that mansion period.
3. Allow user to manually switch to another planet if desired.

## 2. Planet Profiles (General – for everyone)

### SATURN

* **Keywords:** discipline, limits, patience, responsibility
* **Colors:** black, dark blue, dark brown
* **Scents:** myrrh, cypress, vetiver, patchouli, sage
* **Supportive foods:** warm root vegetables, whole grains (barley, oats, rye), sesame, dark leafy greens
* **Avoid foods:** heavy junk food, extreme restriction, skipped meals
* **Fasting instruction:**

  * “Fast from distraction and excess: keep food simple, no sugar/junk, reduce entertainment.”
* **Service instruction:**

  * “Finish a pending task, honor a commitment, or support someone older/long-term in your life.”
* **Behavior focus:**

  * “Be consistent, sober, and reliable.”
