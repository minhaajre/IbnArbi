Build a **Chinese Feng Shui Time Energy module** that classifies the quality of time for **activity planning** (research, learning, business, construction, travel, strategy, etc.).

The system should work similarly to **Vedic Nakshatra logic**, but using **Chinese Feng Shui calendrical variables**.

Do NOT implement personal astrology (BaZi charts, personality analysis, compatibility, etc.).
This module should only analyze **cosmic time conditions**.

---

# 1. Core Concept

The system evaluates the **energy of a given date** using Chinese metaphysical calendar systems.

The output should classify whether the time is suitable for activities such as:

* research
* learning
* writing
* business
* trade
* negotiation
* travel
* construction
* planning
* spiritual work
* healing
* strategy
