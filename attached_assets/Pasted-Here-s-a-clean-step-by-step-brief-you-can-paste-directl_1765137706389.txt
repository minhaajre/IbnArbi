## 2. Enhancements to the Hour Detail View

For the screen/card that shows “Hour of X” (Mars, Venus, etc.):

### 2.1 Add a “Meaning of This Hour” card

Under the main hour title and time range, add a new info card:

* Title: `Meaning of this hour`
* Content: short paragraph describing:

  * Emotional signal for this planet
  * What this hour tends to open or test
  * One sentence of adab (how to behave in it)

Example for Mars:

> This is a Majestic hour (jalāl): energy rises, truth cuts through confusion, and impatience may appear. It is good for courage and cutting distractions, but not for impulsive arguments.

### 2.2 Add “Recommended Practice” card

Next to / below it, add another card:

* Title: `Recommended spiritual practice`
* Content: 3–5 bullet points:

  * Suggested dhikr (one Divine Name)
  * Suggested action (journaling, breathwork, brief walk, etc.)
  * One “do” and one “avoid”

Example structure:

* Dhikr: **al-Ḥafīẓ** (The Preserver)
* Do: cut one distraction, focus on what truly matters.
* Avoid: sending messages in anger or haste.

### 2.3 Add a small “Inner State Check” strip

Below the two cards, add a narrow strip:

* Title: `Inner state check`
* Text: one sentence:

> If you feel expansion, this hour supports action. If you feel contraction, stay quiet and let the hour purify you.

This reflects Ch. 390 (Spirit of Time) without going into heavy theory.

## 3. Enhancements to the Mansion Detail View

For the screen/card that shows the current lunar mansion:

### 3.1 Add “Mansion Theme” card

* Title: `Mansion theme`
* Content: 2–3 sentence description:

  * Core theme (e.g. “seed intention”, “stability”, “relationship testing”, “closure”)
  * Whether it tends toward **beginning**, **stabilising**, or **ending** energy.

### 3.2 Add “Good Today For / Not Ideal For” card

Two columns or a bullet list:

* `Good for:` 3–5 items
* `Not ideal for:` 3–5 items

Keep language simple: starting conversations, signing contracts, releasing habits, etc.

### 3.3 Add “Suggested Divine Name & Practice” card

* Title: `Suggested dhikr & practice`
* Body:

  * One Name (e.g. *al-Nūr*, *al-Qawī*, *al-Laṭīf*).
  * One simple practice (dua, journaling, cleaning, charity, extra rakaʿāt, sleep ritual, etc.).

### 3.4 Optional: “Cycle Role” tag

Near the mansion name, show a small label:

* `Seed`, `Build`, `Relate`, or `Release`

This helps users see where they are in the 28-day cycle.
