# **Guide to the 28 Lunar Mansions in the Akbarian System**

The 28 lunar mansions (manāzil al-qamar) are the Moon’s stations as it travels through the sky.
In Ibn ʿArabī’s cosmology, each mansion is not just a region of space but a **place where a specific divine quality enters time**, shaping events, moods, and spiritual openings.

This guide explains:

* the meaning of each mansion
* what it is good for
* what to avoid
* how waxing or waning moon affects it
* what kinds of relationships it supports
* what kinds of beginnings or endings it favors
* suggested divine Names and ritual directions

This system is meant to help you recognize the **energy of the day**, align with it, and use it wisely.

---

# **How to Use This Guide**

Each day, check:

1. **Which mansion the Moon is in**
2. Whether the Moon is **waxing or waning**
3. Whether you need a **beginning**, **continuation**, or **closure**
4. Whether you want to support **friendship**, **healing**, **creation**, or **release**
5. Which **divine qualities** the mansion brings

Each mansion has its own “flavor.”
Some open doors.
Some stabilize.
Some test relationships.
Some help you close cycles gracefully.

This guide shows you how to work with all of them.

---

# **The Four Categories of Mansions**

To make the system simple and intuitive, the 28 mansions are grouped into four functional categories.
These categories are based directly on Ibn ʿArabī’s spiritual hierarchy of existence.

### **1. Initiating Mansions (1–4)**

These carry “seed” energy—clarity, intention, raw potential.
Good for beginning anything that needs vision or insight.

### **2. Stabilizing Mansions (5–8)**

These give form, structure, and embodiment.
Good for building, committing, and strengthening.

### **3. Relational & Cosmic Mansions (9–20)**

These deal with leadership, communication, travel, learning, emotion, creativity, challenges, and growth.

### **4. Threshold Mansions (21–28)**

These deal with endings, karmic lessons, spiritual openings, hidden forces, and transitions.
Good for closure, healing, and inner work.

# **Waxing vs Waning: How It Changes the Mansion**

### **Waxing Moon (New → Full)**

* Best for **starting** things
* Supports growth, attraction, gathering
* Opens doors outwardly

### **Waning Moon (Full → New)**

* Best for **ending** things
* Supports release, healing, forgiveness
* Closes unnecessary loops and clears paths

Each mansion in this guide will tell you how it behaves differently depending on waxing or waning.
