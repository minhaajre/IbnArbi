## II. The 28 Mansions: Spirit & Ink Framework

Each mansion has a specific "Nature" that dictates the type of ink and the spirit involved. According to Al-Buni, the **Letter** is the soul of the mansion.

### The "Ink Rule" of Al-Buni:

* **For Sa’d (Fortunate) Mansions:** Use "Living Ink" made of Saffron, Rosewater, and Musk.
* **For Nahs (Malefic) Mansions:** Use "Dark Ink" made of soot, vinegar, or juices from bitter plants (like Aloe).
