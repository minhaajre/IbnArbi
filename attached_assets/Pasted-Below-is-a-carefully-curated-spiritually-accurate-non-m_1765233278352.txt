# **ADVANCED SUFI AẒKĀR & LITANIES (CURATED LIST)**

### *Structured by spiritual nature, not by talismanic claims*

Below is a list of the most widely-recognized and spiritually safe a̧wrād within classical Sufism.

---

# **1. Hizb al-Baḥr (The Litany of the Sea)**

**By:** Abū’l-Ḥasan al-Shādhilī
**Nature:** Protection, surrender, reliance, expansion of heart
**Best aligned with:**

* Moon hours (reflection, surrender)
* Jupiter hours (openness, trust, generosity)
* Mansions associated with journeying, transitions, travel
* Days of important thresholds or new beginnings

**Akbarian rationale:**
Trust in divine unfolding; dissolving anxiety about outcomes; strengthening tawakkul.

App phrasing should be:

> “This hour may harmonize with recitations emphasizing trust, such as Hizb al-Baḥr.”

---

# **2. Qasīdat al-Burda (The Mantle Poem)**

**By:** Imam al-Būṣīrī
**Nature:** Praise of the Prophet ﷺ, healing of the heart, softening
**Best aligned with:**

* Venus hours (gentleness, beauty)
* Moon hours (compassion, reflection)
* Mansions of reconciliation, healing, relationship harmony
* Fridays

**Akbarian rationale:**
It opens the heart to beauty (jamāl) and mercy (raḥma), essential for maintaining adab of the moment.

App phrasing:

> “A time associated with beauty and gentleness; some may find resonance in reciting Qasīdat al-Burda.”

---

# **3. Hizb al-Waﬁ (Hizb al-Wiqāya / Hizb al-Nawawi)**

**By:** Imam al-Nawawī
**Nature:** Spiritual grounding, clarity, protection
**Best aligned with:**

* Saturn hours (boundaries, seriousness)
* Mars hours (courage with restraint)
* Mansions related to stabilizing or closing cycles

Akbarian rationale: Strengthens inner composure (sukūn al-qalb).

---

# **4. Ḥizb al-Naṣr (Litany of Victory)**

**By:** Various Shadhili and Jazuli lineages
**Nature:** Inner fortitude, endurance, establishing clarity
**Best aligned with:**

* Mars hours (decisive clarity, not aggression)
* Jupiter hours (expansive openings)
* Mansions of establishment, firmness

Important:
NOT for “victory over enemies” but for **victory over confusion and inner fragmentation**.

App phrasing:

> “This time may harmonize with litanies emphasizing strength and clarity, such as Ḥizb al-Naṣr.”

---

# **5. Wird al-Laṭīf**

**By:** Imam al-Ḥaddād
**Nature:** Gentle protection, ease, removal of constriction
**Best aligned with:**

* Mercury hours (subtle shifts)
* Venus hours (grace)
* Mansions of communication, relationship, reconciliation

Akbarian rationale:
Supports the seeker in periods of contraction (qabḍ).

---

# **6. Dalā'il al-Khairāt**

**By:** Imam al-Jazūlī
**Nature:** Praise upon the Prophet ﷺ, opening of inner lights
**Best aligned with:**

* Sun hours (illumination)
* Fridays
* Mansions associated with inspiration or openings

Akbarian rationale:
Tajalliyāt al-nubuwwa (prophetic illuminations) harmonize with hours that carry meanings of light.

---

# **7. Hizb al-Kabīr (Sheikh al-Akbar’s Prayer Cycle)**

**By:** Ibn ʿArabī himself
**Nature:** Deep remembrance, clearing of inner veils
**Best aligned with:**

* Any hour of expansion
* Mansions of release or unveiling

This is one of the few authenticated litanies from Ibn ʿArabī.

App phrasing:

> “This moment may support deeper remembrance; some seekers recite litanies attributed to Ibn ʿArabī such as Hizb al-Kabīr.”

---

# **8. Hizb al-Dawr al-Aʿlā (The Supreme Cycle)**

**Attributed to:** Ibn ʿArabī
**Nature:** Invocation of divine Names in cosmological sequence
**Best aligned with:**

* Sun hours (centrality)
* Jupiter hours (expansion)
* High-energy mansions (openings, alignments)

This is the closest thing in Sufi tradition to a “cosmic alignment prayer,” but should be framed **only as dhikr**, never as manipulation of forces.

App phrasing:

> “This hour, associated with expansive qualities, may resonate with litanies such as al-Dawr al-Aʿlā.”

---

# **9. Surah al-Wāqiʿah, Yā-Sīn, al-Mulk**

**Nature:**

* Wāqiʿah → provision
* Mulk → protection
* Yasin → inner ease

**Best aligned with:**

* Moon or Venus hours (softening)
* Jupiter hours (expansion)
* Mansions of sustenance, stability, or guidance

Use only as *recommended remembrance*, not “timed results.”

---

# **10. Istighfār (Astaghfirullāh) in Mercury or Saturn hours**

**Nature:** Clearing, resetting, lightening the load
**Best aligned with:**

* Mansions of release
* Waning moon
* Saturn hours (introspection)

Akbarian rationale:
Removes subtle veils that prevent witnessing the hour’s meaning.
