SECTION 4 — WAXING & WANING MOON RULES

WAXING MOON (New → Full):
Use for: abundance, growth, attraction, opportunities, clarity
Dhikr to favor: Jupiter, Venus, Moon, Mercury Names
Avoid: banishing, cutting, Mars/Saturn intensity
Best actions: start things, send applications, plan, build

WANING MOON (Full → New):
Use for: releasing, cleansing, protection, endings
Dhikr to favor: Saturn & Mars Names (in small doses)
Avoid: love rituals, wealth manifestation
Best actions: break habits, detox, cut ties
